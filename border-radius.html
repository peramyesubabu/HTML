Border-Radius property can have 1-4 values:

1.Border-radius with one value (100px):This Value will get applies for all the four corners

2.Border-radius with two values:(100px 50px): First value(100px) applies at top-left and bottom-right corner.second value(50px) will applies at top-right corner and bottom-left corner.

3.Border-radius with three values:(0px 100px 50px): First value(0px) applies at top-left corner.second value(100px) applies at top-right corner and bottom-left corner.Third value(50px) applies at bottom-right corner.

4.Border-radius with four values:(0px 100px 50px 30px): First value(0px) applies at top-left corner.second value(100px) applies at top-right corner third value applies at bottom-right corner and fourth value(30px) applies at bottom-left corner.
